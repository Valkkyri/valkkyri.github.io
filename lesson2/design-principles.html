elements of design

Hick's Law

Minimalism

Blizzard Tecnica

This site does a good job of simplifying the choices that the user can make, which removes some of the friction users can experience when visiting a website. The minimalistic design encourages the user to continue browsing instead of leaving the site.

Fitt's Law

Target Size

KTM Motorcycles

The front page, above the fold of this site does a good job of directing the visitor to click on the button. Not only is it nicely sized, it's also a bright, attractive color that entices the user to click. I also would like to point out the placement of the button, it is within easy thumb reach, making it easier to physically touch the button.

Whitespace &

Clean Design

Sage Flyfishing

I really like the use of whitespace and clean design on this front page. There's lots of whitespace around the text. Not only is there space created around the actual content of the page, but the background image also gives the visitor a sense of room and a the feeling of being out on the river.
