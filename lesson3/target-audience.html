Target Audience

Audience 1 - Mothers

Personal
  • Age: 30’s – 50’s
  • Gender: Female
  • Education: Highschool and some or more College
Professional
  • Work: None – Part-Time
  • Professional background: Homemaker, or other local hospitality or retail work.
  • Why visit the site: To check weather in the area when planning outings with their children or friends.
  • Alternative source information or services: Built in Weather app on a mobile device, the news, or from a digital assistant.
  • When will users access the site? A day or two before their plans (or day of if it looks sketchy outside), possibly a week before an event.
  • Where will users access the site? On a computer or mobile device.
Technical
  • What technological devices does your person use on a regular basis? Mobile phone.
  • What software and/or applications does your person use on a regular basis? Texting, photos, calendar, Social Media
  • Through what technological device does your user primarily access the web for information? Mobile phone or computer.
  • How much time does your person spend browsing the web every day? At least 1 hour a day.

Persona 1 - Mothers

Persona group: Mother

Fictional name: Serene Johnson

Job titles and major responsibilities: Mother, taking care of her children

Demographics: 38 years old, Bachelor Degree, Caucasian, and Married

Goals and tasks when visiting the site: To ensure weather will be good for outdoor educational and recreational activities for her children.

Environment: Home, Kids School and Activities. Partially comfortable with technology, she can use her phone for the basic tasks she needs to accomplish, but when asked to do something new, can become flustered. Spends most of her time with her family (mostly children) and friends.

Quote: What’s the weather supposed to be for our field trip (activity) on Monday?

Scenarios:

  • Serene is getting ready for a school outing, she needs to know what the weather will be like so she can dress her kids accordingly and bring any additional supplies they might need.
  • Serene's children have a day off of school in the late spring, she wants to take them somewhere for fun, but isn't sure what the weather will be and what types of activities are available around town.
  • Serene is planning a family reunion. She is checking the weather in advanced, but also needs to have a backup in case of rain or windy conditions. She needs to see what avenues are available for alternate weather.

Audience 2 - Outdoor Recreationalists

Personal
  • Age: 20’s – 50’s
  • Gender: Male
  • Education: Highschool and some or more College
Professional
  • Work: Part-Time / Full Time
  • Professional background: Full-time employee or employer
  • Why visit the site: To check weather in the area when planning hunting/camping/boating trips.
  • Alternative source information or services: Built in Weather app on a mobile device, the news, or from a digital assistant.
  • When will users access the site? A week before their planned trip, as well as during the week leading up to it.
  • Where will users access the site? On a computer or mobile device.
Technical
  • What technological devices does your person use on a regular basis? Mobile phone & computer.
  • What software and/or applications does your person use on a regular basis? Email, texting, photos, calendar, Social Media, as well as gaming and sports apps.
  • Through what technological device does your user primarily access the web for information? Mobile phone or computer.
  • How much time does your person spend browsing the web every day? 2-3 hour a day.

Persona 2 - Hunters

Persona group: Hunter

Fictional name: Mark Taylor

Job titles and major responsibilities: Main income earner

Demographics: 32 years old, Bachelor Degree, Mixed Ethnical Group, and Married

Goals and tasks when visiting the site: Checking the weather in the area he plans to go hunting, in order to make sure he’s got the right gear for his trip.

Environment: Work, Outdoors, Home. Comfortable with technology Spends most of his time while not at work with his family and in outdoor activities.

Quote: What do I need to bring on my trip?

Scenarios:

  • Mark is excited for his hunting trip, he needs to have an idea of what the weather looks like, so he can bring the right kind of gear.
  • Mark has heard of a few places that might be good to hunt in the lower east Idaho area, but needs more information of what the terrain might be like in case of bad conditions.
  • Mark needs to know what types of ammenities are available in the area surrounding the area he wants to hunt so that he knows where he can fuel up and buy snacks or even a meal.
